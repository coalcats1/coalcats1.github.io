Home Made Lasagna

To make lasagna you will need lot of ingreediants:

Meat sauce

  • 1lbs of ground beef (or italian sausege)
  • 32oz of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of water

Cheese mixture

  • 32oz of ricotta chese( or cottage cheese)
  • 3 cups shreeded motzerella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2tsp of dried parsley
  • salt (you choose quantity
  • ground black peper (again you choose)

Don't forget however that you need to have 9 12oz lasagna noodles, no boil. Also herbs and other unspecifide spices can be added for flavor and tampering is always encouraged.

First to make this you need to brown the hamburger, then mix the ricotta and the eggs and half the mozzerlla. Then in the pan pour half a cup of sauce and a layer of noodles, then spread 1/3 of the cheese mix on the noodles. Sprinkle 1/3 of the hamburger meat on top and repeat 2 more times. Add the remaining noodles on top as well as the mozzerella and you should be done. then cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for an hour untill it is 165 degrees internally.
