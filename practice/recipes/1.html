Home made Lasagna

To make lasagna you will need lot of ingreediants:

Meat sauce

  • 1lbs of ground beef (or italian sausege)
  • 32oz of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of water

Cheese mixture

  • 32oz of ricotta chese( or cottage cheese)
  • 3 cups shreeded motzerella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2tsp of dried parsley
  • salt (you choose quantity
  • ground black peper (again you choose)

Don't forget however that you need to have 9 12oz lasagna noodles. Also herbs and other unspecifide spices can be added for flavor and tampering is always encouraged.
