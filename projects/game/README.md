#Story

In my story I think I shall have the player awake in a white void with large shardes of stained glass with hazy images behind them. By entering the stained glas you may access new rooms your goal is to understand where you are, you the player are dead. You aren't in haven or hell you are in the beyond, the compliment of nothingness. because of this no matter what you do your sanity will disolve as you go insane and lose sense of self, till you are not.
